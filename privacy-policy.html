Privacy Policy

📅 Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of April 13, 2026 and applies to the BANA iOS mobile application ("the App") operated by the Bunts Association of North America ("BANA", "we", "us", or "our").

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains clearly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use the BANA app:

Account Information

When you sign in using Microsoft (via Microsoft Authentication Library), we receive your name, email address, and profile picture as provided by Microsoft. We use this solely to create and manage your account within the app.

Event & Chat Content

Messages sent within the app's chat features and interactions with event content are processed to deliver the service. We do not use chat content for advertising purposes.

Device & Usage Data

We may collect non-personally identifiable information such as device type, operating system version, and general app usage patterns to improve the app's performance and user experience.

🔐 Device Permissions

The BANA app requests access to the following device features. Access is only used for the purposes described, and you will be prompted by iOS before any permission is granted:

📷 Camera

Used to scan QR codes at BANA events and to capture event photos. Camera access is only activated when you initiate a scan or photo capture.

📍 Location

Used while the app is open to display nearby events and show your position on the convention map. We do not track or store your location in the background.

🎙️ Microphone

Used for audio-related features during BANA events such as voice interaction. The microphone is only accessed when you actively use audio features.

🔒 No Tracking

We do not track you across other apps or websites. We do not share your device identifiers with advertising networks.

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account securely
  • Display personalised event schedules, maps, and content
  • Enable communication between attendees through the in-app chat
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs based on usage patterns
  • Send event-related notifications (only if you opt in)

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the BANA event experience.

🔗 Third-Party Services

The BANA app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Microsoft Authentication (MSAL) — used for secure sign-in. Subject to Microsoft's Privacy Statement.
  • Apple Services — the App is distributed through the Apple App Store and subject to Apple's standard platform practices.

We do not integrate advertising SDKs, third-party analytics platforms, or data brokers.

🗄️ Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your account information for as long as you have an active account with the BANA app. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please contact us at the email address below and we will process your request within 30 days.

Location data is not stored — it is used in real time and discarded when you close the map view.

👶 Children's Privacy

The BANA app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

✅ Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the BANA app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
